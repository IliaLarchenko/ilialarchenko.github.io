About

I'm Ilia Larchenko — Director of Data Science at Agoda in Bangkok, with 14 years across DS, ML and analytics. My teams ship high-load production systems for pricing and bidding optimization, customer-behavior and LTV prediction, agentic AI services and other projects. In my free time I work on robotics, ML and AI side projects.

In the past I was top-20 globally on Kaggle, more recently I won 1st places in robotics competitions: BEHAVIOR-1K (NeurIPS 2025) and LeHome (online round, ICRA 2026).

I post videos on YouTube and ship code on GitHub. I like to share my knowledge and explain complex technical problems in a clear and simple way.

LeHome Challenge

1st place in the online round of the LeHome Challenge (ICRA 2026). The task was to train a bimanual robot in simulation to fold 4 different types of garments.
